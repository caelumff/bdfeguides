# EX Weapons
1. Special weapons that only come in Elemental Gacha.
2. 4.167% to pull each type of EX Weapon when you happen to pull a 5-star (6%).
3. Comes with 1 auto, 1 special, 1 empty special slot, 2 inheritance slots
4. Stats are zero if not placed in correct slots.
5. Both special slots can be replaced by another special, having 2 effective specials on one weapon.
6. Be mindful that specials lose their innate awakening and slot effects when inherited. (i.e. Attacking/Healing specials will usually end up with lower power)
7. For those effective against weakness elements, they mean fire weapons against wind enemies and so on.
